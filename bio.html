Starting my journey

Growing up I had a large amount of exposure to computers as I’m sure most kids at my age did around that time. I have fond memories of playing computer games such as Heart of darkness and the Tomb Raider series, as such I have developed an interest in anything computer related through the exposure to video games and PC gaming at a young age.

As such when given the option I chose to study subjects such as ICT at GCSE and then computing at A-level. This has then lead me to where I ‘am now which is studying Computer network engineering at Northampton University with the hopes of eventually finding a job in the computing industry.

In my free time, I simply like to catch up with friends although I hope to one day try my hand at animation which if it turns out to be a good fit I may take up as a hobby
